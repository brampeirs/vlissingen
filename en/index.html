Area

Just behind the boulevard of Vlissingen, hidden among the trees, you will find the brand new villas of the luxury Noordzee Resort Largo City. The fresh, bright houses are facing south and have their own terrace where you can relax and enjoy the sun. The wide sandy beach of Vlissingen is just 250 meters away from you and has a Blue Flag - the label for clean and safe beaches and marinas. Spread out your towel on a sunny day, take a refreshing dip in the sea and enjoy a delicious lunch at one of the many beach pavilions. Vlissingen itself is also a wonderful town. Stroll through the charming shops, take a look at the Zeeland maritime “muSeaum”, stroll along the promenade, see the immense ocean-going vessels manoeuvre to the port and dine extensively in one of the many restaurants. No need to get bored. Back at your villa you can end the day relaxing with a glass of wine in front of the crackling fireplace. Now that’s how you go on vacation!
